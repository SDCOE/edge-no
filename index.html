Welcome!

The SDCOE Ed Tech team is excited to present special edTECH-O Tuesdays. The team will co-host 45 minute webinars periodically throughout the school closures. Make sure to subscribe so you don't miss any of the important tips and information.

Teaching Online

We will present an overview and framework for the basics of teaching online. Topics include: building your ecosystem of online instruction, effective tools you to use, online pedagogy - a comparison with F:F teaching, types of communication including how and when they are used, and sharing a strategy for transitioning to online instruction beyond just assigning tasks and activities.

Building an Online Lesson: 
Leveraging the Power of an LMS, Hyperdocs & Online Tools

Featured Webinars

Building an Online Presence & Communicating with Parents and Families

When you teach face-to-face it is easy to build your class presence with students and parents. However, when teaching online one must be more mindful of how they present themselves. Learn to take control of telling your story, about yourself, your classroom, your students, and your community.  We will share how to leverage technology to build your presence as an educator. Tools such as Twitter, Instagram, Google and Facebook will be discussed and how you use them to communicate and interact with students, families and the community.
