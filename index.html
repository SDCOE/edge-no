Welcome!

The SDCOE Ed Tech team is excited to present special edTECH-O Tuesdays. The team will co-host 45 minute webinars periodically throughout the school closures. Make sure to subscribe so you don't miss any of the important tips and information.
The objective of these sessions is to share quick tips with our community in an accessible way. All sessions will be both live and later archived for ample opportunities to view.
Have a need? Please drop us a line in our survey.

Upcoming Webinars

Building Online Lessons Leveraging the Power of Google Classroom, Hyperdocs, and Online Tools 

5/12/20
The Ed Tech team will host a webinar sharing our best thoughts on bulding lesson online for the most student engagement and deepest learning experiences. We will share our tips and tricks for thinking through the process and for integrating resources from multiple sources. Now, it is time to understand and build in the pedagogy behind online teaching. In this course you will be introduced to building and delivering a simple lesson using the Google Suite.

Teaching Online

We will present an overview and framework for the basics of teaching online. Topics include: building your ecosystem of online instruction, effective tools you to use, online pedagogy - a comparison with F:F teaching, types of communication including how and when they are used, and sharing a strategy for transitioning to online instruction beyond just assigning tasks and activities.
