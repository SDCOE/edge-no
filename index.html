Welcome!

The SDCOE Ed Tech team is excited to present edTECH-O Tuesdays. Every other Tuesday from 4 p.m. to 4:45 p.m. the team will co-host a 45 minute virtual session where an educator or student from San Diego County shares something innovative they have done, or are doing, in their classroom or beyond. The objective of these sessions is to share quick tips with our community in an accessible way. All sessions will be both live and later archived for ample opportunities to view.

Sign Up to Present on
edTECH-O Tuesdays

Being a presenter is easy! The Ed Tech team will provide you with a basic presentation template, the Zoom link for your session, and serve as your co-host as you present. We ask that you prepare the slides using the tmplate and provide a basic transcript of what you are going to say during the presentation. We will do the rest.
